## Day 4 — Normal Distribution

- Bell curve / Gaussian distribution
- Symmetric around the mean; mean = median = mode at the peak
- SD controls width: bigger SD = wider + shorter (area always = 1)
- 68-95-99.7 rule:
  - 68% within ±1 SD
  - 95% within ±2 SD
  - 99.7% within ±3 SD
- Anything beyond ±3 SD is rare (~0.3%)
- Shows up everywhere because of the Central Limit Theorem (coming later):
  many small independent random effects add up to a normal distribution
